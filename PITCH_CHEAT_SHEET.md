## 💬 Common Interview Questions

### "How does this compare to existing solutions?"

*Answer:*  
"Most data centers either don't optimize at all, or they focus on just cost or just carbon. EcoDispatch is unique because it:
1. Optimizes both simultaneously
2. Includes real physics models (solar generation, battery degradation)
3. Tests 5 different strategies to find Pareto-optimal solutions
4. Works with imperfect real-world data
5. Provides a live interactive demo so you can see it work"

### "What's the commercial opportunity?"

*Answer:*  
"Three revenue streams:
1. **SaaS licensing** - Energy operators pay per facility
2. **Integration services** - Help enterprises add to their infrastructure
3. **Carbon credits** - Monetize emissions reductions they create"

### "What challenges did you overcome?"

*Answer:*  
"Three big ones:
1. **Data accuracy** - Real solar/demand data is noisy; needed robust modeling
2. **Competing objectives** - Carbon and cost can conflict; solved with multi-objective optimization
3. **Deployment** - Needed to be real-time responsive; solved by caching and vectorized NumPy operations"

### "What would you do next?"

*Answer:*  
"Three phases:
1. **Validate with real operators** - Get actual data center partnerships
2. **Add machine learning** - Learn patterns over time to improve predictions
3. **Integration** - Build SDKs for energy operators' existing systems"
