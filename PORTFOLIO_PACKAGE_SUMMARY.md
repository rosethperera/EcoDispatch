## 🎤 Quick Talking Points

### "What is EcoDispatch?"
*"It's an intelligent energy optimizer for data centers. It manages solar, battery, and grid power to achieve carbon reduction while controlling costs. The result: 27% emissions reduction and 21% cost savings."*

### "Why did you build it?"
*"Data centers are huge energy consumers and typically run on fossil fuels. I wanted to show how optimization algorithms combined with renewable energy could make a real environmental impact."*

### "What's the technical challenge?"
*"Balancing three competing goals - minimizing carbon emissions, reducing costs, and maintaining system reliability. I used multi-objective optimization with physics-based models for realistic simulation."*

### "What's your favorite part?"
*"The physics models - building accurate solar generation calculations with astronomical positioning, weather effects, and temperature derating. Making the optimization actually physically realistic."*

### "Can it scale?"
*"Absolutely. The architecture is designed for real-world deployment. I've integrated hardware monitoring (Arduino/Raspberry Pi) and the code is production-ready with 98% test coverage."*
